Systems Osteology

Integrating multi-omics data to understand bone biology

Systems Osteology is an integrative framework for studying bone biology using multi-scale and multi-omics approaches. By combining spatial transcriptomics, metabolomics, genomics, and computational modeling, systems osteology aims to uncover the complex regulatory networks that control bone development, mineralization, and disease.

This platform provides access to curated datasets and computational resources for researchers interested in bone systems biology.
